About Our Centre

Pioneering holistic development through GNH principles and innovative research

Our Mission

The Centre of Excellence for GNH (Gross National Happiness) Studies and Research at Adamas University is a pioneering initiative aimed at integrating the principles of holistic development and well-being into education, research, and societal progress.

Our Philosophy

Rooted in the philosophy of Gross National Happiness, which originated in Bhutan, the center focuses on fostering sustainable development, cultural preservation, environmental conservation, and good governance. The center offers a multidisciplinary platform for scholars, researchers, and policymakers to explore innovative approaches to happiness and well-being through academic programs, workshops, and collaborative projects.

By promoting a balance between material growth and non-material well-being, the center aligns its mission with global goals for sustainable development and human happiness, making it a unique hub for transformative education and research in India.
